Our Story

At MRG Capital, our founder Manu Rishi Guptha started investing out of necessity - to augment money for education and support his family. What began as a compulsion evolved into a passionate hobby and ultimately a profession. For the last two decades, Manu has been investing for friends and families, building a track record rooted in trust and performance.

Manu firmly believes that portfolio managers claiming to beat Warren Buffett's 20% hurdle should have become richer than Buffett himself. This pragmatic philosophy drives our fundamental principle: capital protection and steady growth over relentless outperformance. An active blogger on leadership, finance, and philosophy, Manu is also a contributor to The Huffington Post. He holds an MBA in Finance from Warwick Business School where he received the Best MBA Citizen Award.

Our SEBI-registered Portfolio Management Services (PMS) and Alternative Investment Funds (AIF) focus on capital preservation principles while delivering superior risk-adjusted returns, helping our clients achieve their financial aspirations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

The minimum investment required for our Portfolio Management Services (PMS) is ₹50 lakhs as per SEBI regulations. For Alternative Investment Funds (AIF), the minimum is ₹1 crore.

Unlike mutual funds, PMS offers personalized portfolio management with direct ownership of securities in your demat account. We customize strategies based on your specific goals, risk appetite, and tax situation. You receive detailed quarterly reports and can interact directly with portfolio managers.

We follow a research-driven, long-term investment approach focused on fundamental analysis. Our strategies emphasize risk management, diversification, and alignment with client goals. We combine quantitative models with qualitative insights to identify high-conviction opportunities.

For PMS, there are no lock-in periods, and you can redeem your investments anytime. However, we recommend a minimum investment horizon of 3-5 years for optimal results. For AIF, there may be specific lock-in periods as per the fund structure.

MRG Capital is SEBI registered (INP000001234) and follows all regulatory guidelines. Your securities are held in your own demat account with a qualified custodian. We have comprehensive risk management frameworks and regular third-party audits to ensure compliance and security.

You will receive detailed quarterly performance reports, monthly portfolio statements, and transaction confirmations. Additionally, you have 24/7 access to your portfolio dashboard and can schedule consultations with your relationship manager anytime.

Yes, absolutely! We work closely with you to understand your financial goals, risk tolerance, time horizon, and preferences. Based on this, we create a customized investment strategy. You can also set exclusions or preferences for specific sectors or stocks.

Taxation depends on the holding period and nature of gains. Long-term capital gains (holdings > 1 year) on equity are taxed at 10% above ₹1 lakh exemption. Short-term gains are taxed at 15%. We provide tax optimization strategies as part of our wealth advisory services.
